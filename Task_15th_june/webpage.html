About Us

Digital art is a form of art made using computers or mobile devices. Artists use special software like Photoshop or Procreate to draw, paint, or design pictures. Unlike traditional art, digital art does not need paper, pencil, or paint. It is clean, easy to edit, and can be shared online quickly. There are many types of digital art, such as pixel art, 3D art, and digital painting.

Some people also use artificial intelligence to create digital pictures. Today, digital art is used in movies, games, websites, and advertisements. Many students and professionals learn digital art to build their careers. It is creative, fun, and full of new possibilities
